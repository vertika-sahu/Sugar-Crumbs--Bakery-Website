~About Us~

Who are we

We are a gourmet family-owned pastry shop, helping customeers to celebrate their special moments for past 8 years. We started in the heart of Kanpur, and at present we factories and retail outlets in numerous cities of Punjab and Uttar Pradesh.

Our Bakery produces delectable pastries that combine classic Asian traditions with modern flavors for the contemporary palette...
Our Bakery provides cutting-edge designed wedding dessert creations using delicious fruits like, apple, strawberries or other fresh fruits to create Mousse cakes, Luscious tarts and a variety of handcrafted Asian pastries specialties.
Our Bakery prides itself on the personalized service it offers to each of its wedding cake and special occasion cake clients. Our decorators use only the freshest and finest ingredients available.

Our Mission- Sugar n Crumbs is committed to provide the best and innovative range of delicious bakery products with outstanding quality & service to keep our customers delighted.
